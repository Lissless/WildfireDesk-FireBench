About Sage

Sage’s mission is to support wildfire recovery by helping people take informed action and work together with their communities to rebuild and move forward.

It is a non-partisan, non-commercial AI assistant focused on practical guidance and community-driven solutions.

How Sage Can Help

Sage helps you understand your options and take practical steps after a wildfire. It supports both individual decisions and community efforts so you can move forward with clarity.

  • Understand your options for FEMA, insurance, and recovery
  • Plan next steps for housing, rebuilding, and navigating systems
  • Organize with neighbors and coordinate community efforts
  • Create materials like meeting agendas, outreach messages, and action plans

Privacy

Your privacy matters. Sage is designed to support you without storing personal information.

  • We do not store your personal data or chat history.
  • Your messages are only used to generate responses during this session.
  • Conversations are not saved, shared, or used for training.
If you are in immediate danger, call 911.

Welcome to Sage

Sage is here to help with wildfire recovery and community action. You can use it to think through next steps, compare options, and create practical materials like emails, plans, or meeting agendas.

How to use Sage

  • Ask a question about recovery, organizing, housing, insurance, or rebuilding.
  • Sage will usually ask a few follow-up questions first so it can tailor the response.
  • Use Grounded in resources for answers based on expert materials and source citations.
  • Use General for a broader response that does not rely on the uploaded documents.

Try asking

  • How do I organize my neighbors to rebuild safely?
  • What should I do if my insurance claim is delayed?
  • Help me draft an outreach message for a community meeting.
