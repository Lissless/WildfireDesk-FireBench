About Sage

Sage’s mission is to support wildfire recovery by helping people take informed action and work together with their communities to rebuild and move forward.

It is a non-partisan, non-commercial AI assistant focused on practical guidance and community-driven solutions.

How Sage Can Help

  • Understand your options for FEMA, insurance, and recovery
  • Plan next steps for housing, rebuilding, and navigating systems
  • Organize with neighbors and coordinate community efforts
  • Create materials like meeting agendas, outreach messages, and action plans

Privacy

Your privacy matters. Sage is designed to support you without storing personal information.

  • We do not store your personal data or chat history.
  • Your messages are only used to generate responses during this session.
  • Conversations are not saved, shared, or used for training.
If you are in immediate danger, call 911.
