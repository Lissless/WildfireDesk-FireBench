2. **Build Relationships**: Establish connections with key stakeholders through meetings, networking events, or community forums. Foster relationships by understanding their roles, responsibilities, and perspectives regarding wildfire mitigation.

5. **Communicate Effectively**: Maintain open lines of communication throughout the process. Regular updates, meetings, and reports can help keep stakeholders informed and engaged.

6. **Leverage Resources**: Identify and seek out resources available through stakeholders, including funding opportunities, expertise, and community support. Collaborate on grant applications or other funding initiatives to address wildfire mitigation needs.
